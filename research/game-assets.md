# Game Assets — Father, Son & Holy Guns

## Game Information

**Title:** Father, Son & Holy Guns
**Genre:** Roguelite, Co-op, Multiplayer, Action
**Platform:** Steam (PC)
**Stage:** Ready to Publish (Demo available on Steam)

## Game Summary

Fast-paced roguelite multiplayer game set in a bizarre world where faith fuels firepower and every run tests your sanity. Join the crusade against aliens as one of the Gunsaders of the Church. Team up with friends online and embark on holy missions to fend off the frantic hordes of cosmic horror, inspired by Lovecraft mythos.

**Tagline:** "When our gods hear no prayer, only bullets are the answer."

## Unique Selling Points

- **4-Player Online Co-op** — Seamless multiplayer through Steam
- **Roguelite Mechanics** — Temporary upgrades per run (Blessings), Black Market mid-run purchases, high replayability
- **Rich Lore & Setting** — Church of Humanity vs Outer Gods, Lovecraftian horror + religious satire
- **Distinct Characters** — 4 unique Gunsaders with different weapons and playstyles (Sister Lilith, Brother Varun, Father Piero, Sister Kamara)
- **Variety of Objectives** — Elimination, Secure Area, Healing, and more mission types
- **Demo Available** — Steam page with playable demo already live

## Game Modes / Features

- **Elimination** — Clear the area of alien forces
- **Secure Area** — Defend The Eldritch Brain during ritual
- **Restore Shrine** — Heal through Yidhra's shrine mechanics
- **Blessings System** — Temporary upgrades per run
- **Black Market** — Mid-run upgrade station for healing and items
